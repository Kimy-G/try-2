About Us

Kasama is a bakery and modern Filipino restaurant from chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores located in the East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago. And was recently award the first Filipino restaurant to earn a Michelin star back in 2023. We are proud of how far we have come and we are thankful to everyone one who has shown nothing but support. We look forward to welcoming you!

Our Goals

Here at Kasama we value our heritage, where we come from, and our family. We are proud to be able to share a piece of our culture with anyone who walks through our doors. And aim to bring the community around us together one meal at a time. Everyone is welcome here at Kasama and to us, everyone is considered a part of our family.
